Q: Is MathUnderground available for iPads and iPhones or for Android?
A: Both! Look us up in the appstore for iOS or Google play for Android.
Q: What kind of math problems are supported with MathUnderground?
A: Currently MathUndergorund supports most of the material taught in 6th-9th grade pre-algebra and algebra I. This includes simplifying expressions like 3*(x+5), through equations with a single unknown to a set of 2-3 equations, quadratic equations, trigonometry, text questions, inequalities, and much more. Future versions will include support to higher grades.
Q: Does MathUnderground cost money?
A: You can download MathUnderground and try it for free. When you run out of credits, you can invite your Facebook friends for more credits and solve your homework for free forever! For the more advanced users, MathUnderground offers questions packages, that are designed to perfect your skills and mathematical intuition.
