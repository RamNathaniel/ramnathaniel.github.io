yHomework support page
Thank you for using yHomework. In this page you can find answers to frequently asked questions, as well as information about yHomework and our plans for the future. Feel free to ask more questions by email at support@yhomework.com and join our community at Facebook, follow us on Twitter or Google+

Q: Is yHomework available for iPhones or for Android?
A: Both! See yhomework.com for the links to the app stores.
Q: What kind of math problems are supported with yHomework?
A: Currently yHomework supports most of the material taught in 6th-8th grade algebra. This includes simplifying expressions like 3*(x+5), through equations with a single unknown to a set of 2 equations. Future versions will include support to higher grades.
Q: Does yHomework cost money?
A: yHomework is free to download on both platforms.
Q: I found a bug in the app - what should I do?
A: If you find a bug please send us a description, along with your phone brand and model, to support@yhomework.com
Q: Will I do better in math thanks to yHomework?
A: If you read through the steps of the solution carefully, and understand each step you will be able to solve math problems yourself.
Q: I really like the Geek! What's his name? Does he have a girlfriend?
A: The geek's name is Virgil. He is not seeing anyone, currently, and is available at virgil@yhomework.com :-)
Q: The app got stuck! What should I do?
A: Older versions of the app sometimes got stuck. If this happens simply uninstall the app and then reinstall it from the app store or play store. This should fix the problem. If the problem persists, email us.
Q: What's the meaning of the name yHomework?
A: When thinking of your math homework, "why" is the first unknwon, and y is usually the second unknown.
