MathMagics support page
Thank you for using MathMagics. In this page you can find answers to frequently asked questions, as well as information about MathMagics and our plans for the future. Feel free to ask more questions by email at mathmagics@yhomework.com and join our community at Facebook, follow us on Twitter or Google+

Q: Is MathMagics available for iPhones or for Android?
A: Both! Look us up in the appstore for iOS or Google play for Android.
Q: What kind of math problems are supported with MathMagics?
A: Currently MathMagics supports most of the material taught in 6th-9th grade algebra. This includes simplifying expressions like 3*(x+5), through equations with a single unknown to a set of 2 equations, quadratic equations, trigonometry, text questions, inequalities, and much more. Future versions will include support to higher grades.
Q: Does MathMagics cost money?
A: Currently MathMagics is completely free, and if you install it now it will stay free for you.
Q: I found a bug in the app - what should I do?
A: If you find a bug please send us a description, along with your phone brand and model, to mathmagics@yhomework.com
Q: Will I do better in math thanks to MathMagics?
A: Yes! Our experience with MathMagics show that students quickly developed their mathematical intuition and rapidly improved their math skills!

Q: The app got stuck! What should I do?
A: Older versions of the app sometimes got stuck. If this happens simply uninstall the app and then reinstall it from the app store or play store. This should fix the problem. If the problem persists, email us.
