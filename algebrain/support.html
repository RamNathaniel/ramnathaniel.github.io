Algebrain - FAQ
Thank you for using Algebrain. In this page you can find answers to frequently asked questions, as well as information about Algebrain and our plans for the future. Feel free to ask more questions by email at algebrain@yhomework.com and join our community at Facebook, follow us on Twitter or Google+

Q: Is Algebrain available for iPhones or for Android?
A: Both! See our home page for the links to the app stores.
Q: What kind of math problems are supported with Algebrain?
A: Currently Algebrain supports most of the material taught in algebra 1. This includes simplifying expressions like 3*(x+5), through equations with a single unknown to a set of 2 equations, quadratic equations, inequalities, and linear and quadratic function graphs. Future versions will include support to more advanced math problems.
Q: Who is Algebrain for?
A: Algebrain is an ads free, and free of marketing gimmicks. It is meant as a learning tool, to be used by both teachers and students.
Q: I found a bug in the app - what should I do?
A: If you find a bug please send us a description, along with your phone brand and model, to algebrain@yhomework.com
Q: Will I do better in math thanks to Algebrain?
A: Yes! If you read through the steps of the solution carefully, we believe that you will come to understand and will be able to solve math problems yourself.
